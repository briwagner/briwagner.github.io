Raindrop

Use Javascript to water the flowers and get a high score!

You are a raindrop. A wonderful unique little raindrop, unlike no other in the sky. And it's your job to deliver your delicious water insides to the flowers below. But how will you choose which petals will grow?

In this project, we tackled creating objects and making them interact. Buttons control the interaction. But next steps would involve using the arrow keys to navigate the raindrop to the ground. Also randomize the score for the flowers. I'd also like to show the flower's value once it is watered.

Cheat Sheet

The only thing worse than being wrong, is being a cheater!

Kidding! Don't mix up your coding syntax just because you're tired, or your memory is gone, or you stayed up too late playing Destiny. Check your chea-... let's call it a crib sheet, and get the code right!

I structured this in Angular.js, as an exercise in learning that framework. It's a neat tool to spit out your data in a structured way without having to fiddle with the html files. Sort of a poor man's CMS. I know just enough to get my data on the page. Angular does a lot more than I can figure out as yet. But I'll get back to it soon.

Fast Football

Avoid the head injuries and play football online!

Here's a game I made to help teach myself Javascript. I borrowed the format from an old tabletop card game that I had as a kid. It was an interesting experience to think about the internal mechanics that are involved, and how to modify the game mechanic for online play.

Video Noter

Take notes, make comments on a video, with time stamps

Making videos is a team effort, and everyone wants to have an opinion on a work in progress. So take notes, make comments, have your voice heard on every second in the video! I built this tool to record comments and time code for a video.

This is built on Popcorn.js, a cool platform for working with video on the web. Thanks, Mozilla. Unfortunately, it seems they've stopped developing it. But the framework is still there, and it's a great base for this tool.
