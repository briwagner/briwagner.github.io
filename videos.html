Iraq Documentary

In 2013, I had the opportunity to go to Iraq and help produce a documentary with a group of Iraqi journalists. The film was about the nation's oil industry and how it has driven many recent historical events. After having made many reporting trips to Latin America, this was my first time in the Middle East.

Miami Critical Mass

When I lived in Miami, the Critical Mass bike ride was becoming a big deal. Like really big. So I wanted to show how big, and do that with some time lapse footage. My cleverest idea was to ride at the front of the pack, stop at an intersection and film the rest of the mass passing by. Well, after a few minutes, the drivers behind me were getting really angry. And the crowd kept going. Too big for me to capture comfortably. But you get an idea, just how many rabble-rousing bike riders Miami has.
